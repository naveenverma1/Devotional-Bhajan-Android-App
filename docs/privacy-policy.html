Privacy Policy — Sunderkand Path and Chalisa

App: Sunderkand Path and Chalisa (com.nv.user.sunderkand)
Publisher: Naveen Apps (Naveen Verma)
Last updated: 25 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sunderkand Path and Chalisa Android application ("the app", "we") handles information when you use it.

1. Summary

Sunderkand Path and Chalisa is a free, ad-free devotional reader and audio player. The app does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal user data. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, no location.

2. Information the app uses

  • Internet access (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE permissions) — used only to fetch the list of available chalisa titles from a public read-only HTTPS endpoint. No request includes any user identifier.
  • No login, no accounts, no profile data.
  • No advertising and no advertising identifier.
  • No location, contacts, photos, or device sensors.

3. Data collection and sharing

None. The app collects no user data and does not share data with any third party.

4. Data retention and deletion

Because the app does not collect or store user data, there is nothing to retain and nothing to delete. Uninstalling the app from Settings → Apps → Sunderkand Path and Chalisa → Uninstall removes all cached app data from your device.

5. Children's privacy

The app contains religious and devotional content suitable for general audiences. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

6. Audio and text content

All audio recordings, chalisa text, and images bundled with the app are provided for personal, non-commercial devotional use. They are not transmitted or analyzed by us.

7. Security

Because no personal data leaves your device under our control, there is no network-transmission risk specific to this app. The list-fetch network request uses HTTPS via the Android system's standard networking stack.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at the same URL with an updated "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy or the app, open an issue on the project repository: https://github.com/naveenverma1/Devotional-Bhajan-Android-App/issues.
